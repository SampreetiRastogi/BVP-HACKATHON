Saarthi Travel Safety Tips

  • Always book through Saarthi-verified operators for tourist safety assurance.
  • Share your live trip status with emergency contacts via the Saarthi app.
  • For remote routes (NE India, Himachal), enable offline SOS mesh mode before boarding.
  • Keep your Digital ID QR accessible — some checkposts may scan it for safety verification.
  • Use 1363 (Tourist Helpline) for any transport-related emergencies across India.
